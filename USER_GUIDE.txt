CAREER CAVES - QUICK USER GUIDE

1) Create an account or sign in
- Open the app in your browser.
- New users: go to Signup and create an account.
    - You will need to use a real email and confirm email from your inbox
    - login for demo if having issues: 
    user: toledov@ufl.edu  
    pass: Albert123
- Returning users: go to Login and sign in.

2) Dashboard
- After login, you land on the Dashboard.
- Use the search bar to filter trending listings by title, category, size, or condition.
- Select a listing card to view listing details.
- Check the weather panel for current conditions and today's high/low in Gainesville.

3) Discover listings
- Open Discover to browse available items from other users with filters
- Review listing details before deciding to message the owner.

4) Create and manage your listings
- Open Lending to create a new listing.
- Add title, price per day, category, size, condition, and optional image.
- Update or remove your listings from the same page when needed.

5) Messaging
- Open Messages to view your conversations.
- Select a conversation and send messages to coordinate rental details.

6) Profile
- Open Profile to review account information and app-related profile details.

Tips
- If pages do not load, confirm backend and frontend dev servers are both running.
- If listing actions fail refresh the page
